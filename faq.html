Orders & Tracking
How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package.

Do I need an account to place an order?

You can browse our products as a guest. However, to complete your purchase and make payment, you’ll need to sign up for an Elora account. Creating an account also allows you to track your orders and enjoy exclusive member perks.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders cannot be changed once placed. However, you may cancel your order within 24 hours by contacting our customer support team. After this period, your order will have entered the processing stage and cannot be canceled.

What should I do if my order is missing or delayed?

If your order is missing or significantly delayed, please contact our customer support team with your order number. We will investigate the issue with our shipping partner and work to resolve it promptly.

Why is my order still pending?

Orders may appear as 'pending' if the payment has not been fully processed. Please check your payment method or contact our support team for assistance.

I made a mistake in my order details. What should I do?

If you entered incorrect information (such as your email or phone number), please contact our customer support immediately so we can assist before the order is processed.
