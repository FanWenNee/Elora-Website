Our Guiding Principles

At the heart of Elora lies a commitment to three core values that define our philosophy and shape every product we create.

Sustainability

We honor our planet through mindful sourcing, eco-conscious packaging, and ethical practices. Beauty should never compromise our Earth's wellbeing.

  • Responsible ingredient sourcing
  • Recyclable & biodegradable packaging
  • Carbon-neutral initiatives

Natural

Pure botanical ingredients form the foundation of our formulations. We harness nature's wisdom to create effective, authentic skincare solutions.

  • Botanical-powered formulations
  • No harsh synthetic additives
  • Transparent ingredient disclosure

Gentle

Every formulation respects your skin's delicate balance. We create products that nurture, protect, and care for all skin types with tenderness.

  • Dermatologically tested
  • Suitable for sensitive skin
  • Non-irritating formulations

These principles guide our journey, ensuring every Elora product delivers not just beauty, but integrity, purity, and care.

Our Philosophy

Our History

Founded in 2022 by Kitty and Zoey in Kepong, Kuala Lumpur, Elora was born from the belief that every woman deserves to feel confident and discover her own beauty. Our mission is simple: skincare crafted with natural ingredients, made for all skin types, designed to care, and created to reveal every woman’s best look.

Inspired by Kitty’s struggle with sensitive skin and years of unsuitable products, Elora began with a gentle Cleansing Milk and has since grown into a range that nurtures, protects, and empowers women to embrace their natural glow.

Our Commitment

At Elora, we are dedicated to creating skincare and makeup that truly care. Our products are made with natural ingredients and gentle formulas, ensuring they are safe and effective for all skin types. We believe in transparency, sharing openly about what goes into every product so you can trust what touches your skin.

We are committed to sustainability, from mindful ingredient sourcing to eco-conscious packaging, because beauty should never come at the planet’s expense. Above all, we strive to inspire confidence and help reveal everyone’s best look — enhancing natural beauty, never masking it.

Secret of the Beauty

At Elora, we believe true beauty starts with nature. That’s why our products are powered by botanical and natural ingredients, carefully chosen for their purity and effectiveness. Every formula is crafted with care to be gentle on all skin types, bringing balance, nourishment, and long-lasting results.

One of our signature ingredients is Niacinamide, a powerhouse vitamin we include in many of our products. Known for its ability to brighten, smooth, and strengthen the skin barrier, Niacinamide works in harmony with other natural extracts to enhance your glow. Combined with our thoughtful formulations, it is part of our promise to deliver skincare that cares — naturally.

Physical Outlet

While we've expanded our online presence, our physical boutiques remain the heart of the Elora experience. Each location is designed as a sanctuary where you can explore our products, receive personalized consultations, and experience the full range of our skincare and beauty offerings.

Our trained beauty advisors are available to help you find the perfect products for your skin type and concerns. We believe in creating meaningful connections with our customers and providing an exceptional in-store experience that reflects the quality of our products.

Current Locations: Pavillion Kuala Lumpur, One Utama, Ipoh Parade
